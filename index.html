I am currently a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Indiana University Bloomington (IUB) advised by Prof. Haixu Tang. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology from Xidian University, China in July 2019. From September 2019 to July 2020, I studied as a graduate student at Xi'an Jiaotong University, China.

My research interests focus on deep learning and its applications in bioinformatics, especially molecular representation learning and mass spectrometry related tasks.
